Curriculum Vitae

Maria Gwyn McDowell is a scholar with a penchant for ministry, a theologian with an inclination for practice. She is feminist, a student of liberation theology, a scholar of Ethics and Eastern Christianity. She is an active member of The Episcopal Church, and currently a Candidate for Ordination to Holy Orders in the Diocese of Oregon. She received her PhD in Theological Ethics from Boston College and writes on gender, sexuality and women in the church. She is an irregular lover of God and God's emerging creation, and a fervent heckler of all teams which oppose the Rose City Thorns.
